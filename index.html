Staff Notices

It's Release Day!!

Today marks a huge moment for all of us. After weeks of planning, building, testing, and pushing through every challenge that came our way, the wait is finally over. Frontline London officially opens its doors today, and what was once just planning documents, development chats, and many failed servers— now becomes a fully operating server open to the public.

After days of hard work and relentless effort, our development team has pieced this project into something we can all be proud of. It hasn’t been easy—from bugs and balancing to late nights and unexpected setbacks—our team has worked tirelessly to ensure every detail is ready for launch.

While we celebrate today’s launch, please remember that this is just the beginning. There will be challenges and bugs along the way; your feedback, reports, and patience are crucial as we continue to refine the server. Thank you for being here, and let’s build Frontline London together.
